# The Adventure Begins

Last weekend, a few friends and I packed our bags and headed to Sabah. Our goal? Conquering **Mount Kinabalu**. It was an experience I'll never forget!

![Mountain View](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1464822759023-fed622ff2c3b?q=80&w=1200&auto=format&fit=crop)

## The Ascent

The hike was gruelling but beautiful. The air got thinner as we pushed through the tree line and onto the bare granite rock faces. 

We started our final ascent at 2:00 AM to catch the sunrise, and let me tell you, the cold was biting. But nothing beats the feeling of standing at Low's Peak as the sun breaks over the horizon, painting the clouds in gold and purple.

### Good Food, Good Friends

After the descent, we rewarded ourselves with some amazing local seafood in Kota Kinabalu. It's moments like these that remind me to step away from the keyboard and explore the world.

Can't wait for the next trip!
